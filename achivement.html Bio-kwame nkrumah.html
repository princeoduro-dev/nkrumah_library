Achievement

Kwame Nkrumah accomplished much for Ghana and Africa. Some of his accomplishments are listed below.

  • Akosombo Textiles Limited
  • Kumasi Shoes Factory
  • Tema Motorway
  • Komeda Sugar Factory
  • Bolgatanga Meat Processing Factory
  • Nsawam Cannery Factory
  • Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology

Ghana gained independence in 1957 from the British under Kwame Nkrumah.

In 1962, Kwame Nkrumah was photographed with Ghanaian footballers.
